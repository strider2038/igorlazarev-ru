This site uses minimal web analytics and an external comments widget to understand which articles are read more often and to support discussions below posts.

## Yandex Metrica

This site loads Yandex Metrica only after analytics cookie consent. The counter is not loaded before the visitor makes a choice.

Metrica may process technical visit data: cookie identifiers, IP address, browser user-agent, page URL, referrer, visit time, and page interactions. This data is used for traffic statistics and site improvement.

## giscus Comments

Comments are powered by giscus and GitHub Discussions. The comments widget is loaded only after consent for functional cookies and external widgets.

If you leave a comment through giscus, data processing is also governed by GitHub's rules.

## Cookies

The site stores a cookie with consent preferences so that the choice is not requested on every page view. Analytics cookies are used only after analytics consent. Functional cookies and external widgets are used only after consent for the functional category.

You can change your choice through the "Cookie settings" link at the bottom of the page or by clearing this site's cookies in your browser.

## External Links And Materials

Regular links to GitHub, Telegram, YouTube, and other platforms do not transfer data to those platforms until you follow the link.

Some materials may include embedded videos or external content. Such embeds may contact third-party services when the page loads.

## Contact

For privacy questions, you can contact the author through Telegram or GitHub; links are available on the About page.
